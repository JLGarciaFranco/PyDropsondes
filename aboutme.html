Short biography

I am originally from Mexico City, where I studied my Bachelor of Science in Earth Sciences at Faculty of Sciences of the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM). Since my early undergraduate days, the atmosphere caught my eye, attention and interest and I have been passionate about most atmospheric phenomena ever since. I have participated in four different research projects in mixed-layer and precipitation spectra in Mexico, land-atmosphere interaction in Canada and finally Tropical Cyclones in the UK.
